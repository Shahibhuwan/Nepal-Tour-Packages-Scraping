    Overview

    A place to visit on your awaited vacation full of natural beauty and ancient culture. Nepal is a country among many other south Asian countries in Asia and one of the most loved and repeatedly visited countries worldwide. Home to thousands of Hindu deities this country is ancient and a holy land for Hindus. Visitors can witness sadhus sitting on the bank of Bagmati river and talk to them about life. This country is one of the places you must visit before you die. The Nepal itinerary 7 days shall help you plan a trip for yourself and your family.

    This country was full of small kingdoms until late king Prithvi Narayan Shah unified it and protected it from the colonization of the British East India company. He united this God forsaken land by conquering different kingdoms with different cultures. Hence, visitors can witness hundreds of cultures in an area of 147,181 sq. Km. Such as Tamang, Gurung, Tharu, Maithili and much more. There are many sacred lands, and holy places in Nepal for tourists and Lumbini is one of them. Lumbini is the birthplace of Siddhartha Gautam also known as Lord Buddha. He was born in 623 B.C. in the land of Lumbini which is now a famous pilgrimage site for people from all over the world. Sacred gardens, pillars established by King Ashoka of India in his honor can still be found and witnessed on your Nepal itinerary 7 days. Religion and culture attract many tourists, but Nepal is also known worldwide for its natural beauty. In the Himalayan region of Nepal, some villages are untouched by modernization and tourists visit these villages to witness the simple yet straightforward lifestyle and traditional food of these villagers who welcome every tourist with love and affection. This country is renowned throughout the world for its mountains. Some of the tallest mountain peaks in the world are located here including Mt.Everest “the tallest mountain in the world”, which was first climbed by Tensing Norgay Sherpa and Sir Edmund Hillary in the early 50s. Since then thousands of tourists have visited and climbed this peak and captured some of the best views from the world highest mountain. Kanchenjunga, Lhotse, Annapurna are some of the other eight-thousanders of Nepal.

    Heaven on earth is what many tourists say when departing from this country with beautiful memories and a lasting impression. The wait is over, visit and experience it all in your Nepal seven days family trip.

      FAQs for Nepal

      Why Nepal is called the Himalayan nation?

      Nepal is called Himalayan nation because there are the many Himalayas and the world tallest Himalaya Mount Everest also lies in Nepal.

      What are the main tourist attractions spot in Nepal?

      Kathmandu, Bhaktapur, Pokhara, Chitwan national park, Langtang region, Swayambhunath temple, Boudhanath Stupa, Lumbini, Everest base camp, etc are some of the tourist spots in Nepal.

      What are the rare species that you can see while traveling Nepal?

      The red panda, One horn rhino, Bengal tiger, Asiatic Elephant, Asiatic Elephant, Clouded leopard, Giant hornbill, etc are some of the rare species that you can see in Nepal.

      Is there any beach in Nepal?

      Nepal is landlocked country so there is no beach in Nepal.

      What is famous in Nepal?

      Nepal is famous for its unique handicrafts item such as traditional masks, prayer wheels, puppets, carved wooden sculptures, and pottery items.
